Recovery Support Services

$721,000

Our largest investment area focuses on building a comprehensive recovery ecosystem that supports individuals at every stage of their recovery journey.

Peer Support Programs

Trained peers who have lived experience with addiction provide mentorship, guidance, and hope to those in recovery.

  • One-on-one peer mentoring
  • Group peer support sessions
  • 24/7 peer support hotline
  • Family peer support services

Recovery Coaching

Professional recovery coaches help individuals develop personalized recovery plans and navigate the path to sustained wellness.

  • Individual recovery planning
  • Goal setting and accountability
  • Resource navigation
  • Life skills development

Support Groups

Various support group options provide community, connection, and shared learning opportunities.

  • 12-step and alternative programs
  • Family support groups
  • Specialty groups (youth, women, veterans)
  • Virtual and in-person options

Transportation Assistance

Removing transportation barriers to ensure access to treatment and recovery services.

  • Medical appointment transportation
  • Support group transportation
  • Emergency transportation services
  • Bus pass programs

Housing Support

Stable housing is fundamental to recovery. We provide various levels of housing assistance.

  • Recovery housing placement
  • Transitional housing programs
  • Housing navigation services
  • Rent assistance programs

Employment Services

Supporting economic stability through job readiness and employment support.

  • Job readiness training
  • Resume and interview preparation
  • Employer partnerships
  • Vocational training opportunities

Harm Reduction

$254,000

Life-saving interventions that meet people where they are and reduce the immediate risks associated with substance use.

Naloxone Distribution

Free naloxone (Narcan) distribution and training to reverse opioid overdoses.

  • Community naloxone training
  • Free naloxone kits
  • First responder training
  • Leave-behind naloxone programs

Syringe Service Programs

Preventing disease transmission and connecting people to care.

  • Safe syringe exchange
  • HIV/HCV testing
  • Wound care services
  • Linkage to treatment

Overdose Prevention Education

Community education to prevent overdoses and save lives.

  • Overdose recognition training
  • Safe use education
  • Fentanyl test strip distribution
  • Community awareness campaigns

Post-Overdose Response Teams

Rapid response teams that engage overdose survivors when they're most receptive to help.

  • 24-48 hour follow-up visits
  • Immediate treatment linkage
  • Family support services
  • Ongoing case management

Early Intervention

$203,000

Identifying and supporting at-risk individuals before substance use escalates to crisis levels.

Screening and Assessment

Early identification of substance use concerns through evidence-based screening tools.

  • Universal screening programs
  • Comprehensive assessments
  • Youth screening initiatives
  • Primary care integration

Brief Interventions

Short, focused conversations that help individuals recognize and address risky substance use.

  • Motivational interviewing
  • Brief counseling sessions
  • Risk reduction planning
  • Follow-up support

Referral to Treatment

Seamless connections to appropriate levels of care when more intensive support is needed.

  • Warm handoffs to providers
  • Insurance navigation
  • Treatment matching
  • Barrier removal assistance

Prevention Programs

Evidence-based prevention initiatives for schools, families, and communities.

  • School-based prevention
  • Parent education programs
  • Community coalitions
  • Youth leadership development

Additional Support Services

Through our network of partners and MOA funding, we also facilitate access to:

Evidence-Based Treatment

Access to medication-assisted treatment (MAT), counseling, and comprehensive addiction treatment services.

Criminal Justice Programs

Diversion programs, treatment courts, and specialized services for justice-involved individuals.

Reentry Support

Comprehensive support for individuals returning to the community from incarceration.

Family Services

Support for families affected by substance use, including counseling and education.
